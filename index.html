About Me

Hello, welcome to my portfolio page.

A few fun facts about me: I have been self-employed most of my life, running businesses ranging from a one-person operation to larger endeavors where I had 54 people on staff. I have a finance degree and worked as a corporate accountant and financial analyst. This gave me good exposure to IT. I have built websites (primarily from web-builders in the past) and managed marketing programs for my businesses. Later, I attended Duke for its digital media and marketing program. Some of the coding in the projects on this site is from my professors at MIT, where I am enrolled in the full stack development and cybersecurity programs.

Thanks again for stopping by, and please don’t hesitate to contact me with any questions. I am also open to freelancing, collaboration, entrepreneurial endeavors, consulting on an outside vendor basis, or being a value-added equity partner.

This is a work in progress. I plan to update and upgrade as I continue my development journey. Please feel free to stop by again and take a gander at the upgrades.
